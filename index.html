NS→GCal
To use, click this new bookmark when viewing your schedule.
Wait for the blue button to appear in the left sidebar and click it.
A popup window will appear to authorize your Google Calendar account. You only have to do this once.
To sync your workdays anytime, click the bookmarklet when you access your schedule again.
