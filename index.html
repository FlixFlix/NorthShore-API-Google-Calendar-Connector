You can always rename it later, e.g. "NorthShore Sync" or whatever.
Default name is now NS→GCal for brevity.

NS→GCal
To use, click this new bookmark when viewing your schedule. This will run the connector.
Wait for the "Authorize" button to appear in the top area and click it.
A popup window will appear to authorize your Google Calendar account. You only have to do this once.
To sync your workdays anytime, click the bookmarklet when you access your schedule again.

To change the display period after running the connector, you must reload the page. Once you changed the period, the connector can be run again to sync the new dates.
